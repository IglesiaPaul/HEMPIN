What is Hemp'in?

Hemp'in is a curated multi‑brand pop‑up in Bangkok where products with hemp inside are presented and sold. From fashion to beauty, homeware to food & drink — if hemp is part of your product, you belong here. We showcase you during the Asia International Hemp Expo and add your collection to our online marketplace.

  • Showroom retail in central Bangkok
  • Marketplace mini‑shop creation
  • In‑store & online sales operations
  • Press, social & stage visibility
